Building Bridges Across Cultures

The International Student Club (ISC) at Prince Sultan University is a unique social and cultural hub that brings students from all around the world together. Our goal is simple: to create lasting friendships, share cultures, and learn from one another in fun, meaningful ways.

With a thriving community of over 200 members, more than 15 cultural sessions every year, and nearly 10 large-scale international events, ISC is one of the most active and diverse student clubs on campus. Whether through small group activities, collaborative sessions, or big international festivals, ISC is all about celebrating diversity while making sure every student feels included, connected, and inspired.
