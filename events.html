Our Projects

Making a difference through our initiatives

Language Exchange Program

Weekly language exchange sessions where students can practice different languages and learn about various cultures. Our members teach each other their native languages in a fun, supportive environment.

Education Cultural
50+ participants Weekly

International Food Festival

Annual celebration of global cuisine featuring traditional dishes from our members' home countries. Students share their cultural heritage through food, music, and stories.

Cultural Community
200+ attendees Annual

Mentorship Program

Connecting new international students with experienced members for guidance and support. Mentors help newcomers adjust to university life and navigate cultural differences.

Support Leadership
80+ mentees Year-round

Cultural Awareness Workshops

Educational sessions on cultural sensitivity, global issues, and international perspectives. These workshops help build understanding and respect among diverse communities.

Education Awareness
100+ participants Monthly

Study Abroad Support

Assisting students with study abroad applications, visa processes, and cultural preparation. We provide resources and guidance for international educational opportunities.

Support Travel
30+ students Semester-based

International Week

Week-long celebration featuring cultural performances, workshops, and global networking events. This is our biggest event of the year, showcasing the diversity of our community.

Cultural Events
500+ attendees Annual
