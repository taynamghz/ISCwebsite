Our Events

Discover the exciting activities and initiatives that make ISC special

Opening Sessions

Where students get to know each other in fun activities, games, and competitions. Happens once every beginning of year to welcome new members and strengthen bonds.

Welcome Team Building
Beginning of Year All Members

International Dish Party

Party session where students have a session to share cultural dishes. Members bring traditional foods from their home countries and share stories behind each dish.

Cultural Food
Regular Sessions 50+ Participants

Board Game Sessions

Fun and interactive board game sessions where students can relax, have fun, and build friendships through various games from different cultures.

Entertainment Social
Monthly 30+ Players

Street Eats

Big event where a lot of international cuisine restaurants come and give free samples. Students can purchase and try dishes from all around the world.

Major Event International Cuisine
Annual 200+ Attendees

Hakaya Ramadan

Where students get to experience Ramadan vibes and listen to historical and old tales presented by hakawati (storytellers). A unique cultural experience.

Cultural Ramadan
Ramadan Season 100+ Attendees
